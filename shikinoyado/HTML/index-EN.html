Spring Stay

Blooming Season, Heart Finds Peace

With gentle spring sunlight, cherry blossoms and camellias bloom in the inn's garden. The scent of flowers carried on the breeze, gentle winds crossing the river. As winter's silence yields, life awakens. At Shikino Yado in spring, you'll feel a serene calmness and the freshness of new beginnings.

Spring Mist Bath – Cherry Blossoms Floating in Mist

In the soft light of spring dawn, steam rises gently. Bathing while gazing at fresh green leaves, as if becoming one with nature. Your body, having endured the long winter, is gently warmed and healed.

Spring Awakening Tray – Spring's Gifts with Delicate Aroma

Spring's cuisine celebrates the awakening of sea and mountain. Beginning with small dishes highlighting firefly squid, bamboo shoots, and shellfish, we present a colorful array of seasonal vegetables in small portions. Cherry blossom-shaped decorative cuts and pale tableware gently convey spring's arrival. Beautiful to behold, gentle to taste – enjoy this truly spring-like presentation.

Summer Stay

Cool Hours Under Clear Streams and Green Shade

Summer at Shikino Yado is wrapped in deep green and refreshing sounds of flowing water. The cicadas' song is surprisingly soothing, and wind chimes ring softly in the shade away from direct sunlight. Forget the summer heat outside and spend cool, luxurious hours here.

Cool Breeze Bath – Enveloped by Green Shade and Water Sounds

When the night breeze is most pleasant, you may see fireflies dancing above the outdoor bath. By day, the water's surface sparkles reflecting blue sky; by night, stars twinkle through the mist. Soak in warm water to gently heal your body and wash away summer's fatigue.

Learn more about our hot spring

Cool Taste Tray – Summer's Colors, Refreshing and Light

Summer's cuisine balances refreshment with deep flavor. Clear red tuna sashimi paired with fresh vegetables enjoyed as shabu-shabu. Turban shell cooked in its own shell releases an ocean fragrance, bringing summer breeze to your table. Enjoy this refreshing feast that gently soothes your flushed body.

Autumn Stay

Abundant Autumn, Hearts Turned Golden

As trees turn crimson and crisp air touches your cheeks. Shikino Yado in autumn wears the year's deepest colors. At night, insect songs echo and a hearth fire flickers gently, as time flows slowly.

Autumn Foliage Bath – Steaming Mist Colored by Fall

Autumn leaves reflected on the water's surface appear like a painting. The comfort of submerging to your shoulders in warm water while wrapped in cool breezes is autumn's unique luxury. After bathing, enjoy chilled sweets made with local pears and persimmons.

Learn more about our hot spring

Autumn Colors Tray – Abundant Harvest Flavors

Autumn's cuisine presents dishes with quiet depth. Expertly grilled white fish and seasonal vegetable tempura. A delicate green maple leaf placed at the plate's edge reflects autumn's transition. Honoring ingredients' natural flavors while savoring subtlety over grandeur – this is autumn's truly elegant presentation.

Winter Stay

Winter's Warmth Embraced in Silence

As snow falls and silence envelops the world. Shikino Yado becomes a warm haven glowing softly in the silver-white landscape. Hearth fire, steaming mist, and solemn air. Here you'll find the quiet, profound peace befitting year's end.

Snow-Viewing Bath – Standing Alone in the Silver World

The snow-viewing bath is winter's truest treasure at Shikino Yado. Gaze upon snowflakes dancing on the water's surface and surrender to silence. At night, lantern light illuminates the mist, creating a fantastical landscape.

Learn more about our hot spring

Crab Hot Pot Tray – Winter's Flavors, Body and Heart Warmed

Winter's flavor treasure is undoubtedly the crab hot pot. Rich umami-infused broth mingles with plump crab and seasonal vegetables, warming you from your core. This moment spent savoring under clouds of steam is winter's ultimate feast. Enjoy this luxurious winter flavor that brings both warmth and peace to heart and body.
