Room Guide

Garden Room

The room where you can feel the changing seasons most intimately. Open the veranda to reveal our beautifully maintained garden. Spring blossoms, summer greenery, autumn leaves, and winter snow – time seems to flow gently. Enveloped in the fragrance of tatami, gazing at light through the shoji screens, taking a moment to rest after bathing – your heart naturally finds peace. Tea time while viewing the garden is where guests most deeply experience the inn's tranquility.

  • Size: 12 tatami + veranda
  • Amenities: Sink, toilet (heated seat), air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe, Wi-Fi
  • Capacity: 3 guests
  • Rate: ¥20,300/person (includes meals)

Four Seasons Hermitage

The symbolic room of Shikino Yado, bearing our inn's name. Each season, the alcove is adorned with hanging scrolls and flowers that change the room's character with each visit. From the wide veranda, you hear the gentle murmur of the small stream flowing through the inn's heart. Morning birdsong, evening insect songs echo softly – breathing naturally deepens in this silence. While honoring Japanese tradition, the room offers modern comfort, making it ideal for extended stays.

  • Size: 15 tatami + veranda
  • Amenities: Sink, toilet (heated seat), air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe, Wi-Fi
  • Capacity: 4 guests
  • Rate: ¥22,300/person (includes meals)

Moonlight Room

A room created to appreciate the moon – hence its name. As night falls, moonlight softly filters through the shoji screens, illuminating the entire room in its gentle glow, creating moments of serene beauty. Enjoy a solitary cup of sake in silence, or share quiet conversation with a loved one. The interior, with its refined palette of deep black and natural cream tones, evokes modest yet dignified beauty. A special moment designed to savor the night.

  • Size: 10 tatami + veranda + private open-air bath
  • Amenities: Sink, toilet (heated seat), air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe, Wi-Fi, private open-air bath
  • Capacity: 2 guests
  • Rate: ¥18,300/person (includes meals)

Elegance Room

A room pursuing quiet sophistication rather than lavishness. Traditional designs – gold-leaf doors and mother-of-pearl inlays – are subtly woven throughout, while the overall space remains gently refined. Opening the shoji reveals distant mountains beyond. At dusk, the landscape glows in warm orange light, creating a dreamlike scene. Perfect for anniversaries and special occasions, this room is recommended for those seeking calm elegance with a touch of the extraordinary.

  • Size: 14 tatami + veranda + private open-air bath
  • Amenities: Sink, toilet (heated seat), air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe, Wi-Fi, private open-air bath
  • Capacity: 3 guests
  • Rate: ¥21,300/person (includes meals)

Wandering Cloud Pavilion

"Like drifting clouds, let your heart flow freely" – a detached room built on this philosophy. Located a slight distance from the main building, it balances tranquility with openness. Featuring a private open-air bath where the sky's colors and wind sounds seem to unite you with nature. More than just healing travel fatigue, this space helps you forget daily life and center your spirit. Popular with solo travelers and couples seeking quiet conversation.

  • Size: 16 tatami + veranda + private open-air bath
  • Amenities: Sink, toilet (heated seat), air conditioning, TV, refrigerator, safe, Wi-Fi, private open-air bath
  • Capacity: 2 guests
  • Rate: ¥25,300/person (includes meals)
