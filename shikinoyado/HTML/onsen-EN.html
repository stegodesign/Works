Our hot spring uses naturally sourced water from a private spring nestled in the mountains. In spring, fresh greenery frames the baths; in summer, refreshing breezes drift across; in autumn, crimson leaves reflect in the water; and in winter, snow landscapes paint the scenery around the bath.


Our indoor bath is thoughtfully designed with warm wood tones, allowing you to relax comfortably even on rainy days. The open-air bath offers ever-changing seasonal views, and at night the starlit sky spreads above you in its full glory.


Our spring water is "slightly alkaline simple hot spring water." With its gentle touch on skin, it is often called the "beauty bath," welcoming guests of all ages. As it retains warmth well, it warms both body and spirit, soothing away travel fatigue.

    Children of the opposite gender should be approximately 6 years old or younger.
    Parents must always keep a close eye on children for safety.
